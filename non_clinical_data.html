Non-clinical Data (SEND)

At EndPoint Data Analytics, we provide comprehensive SEND-compliant non-clinical data services to support regulatory submissions and streamline agency review. Our experts ensure accurate data standardization, validation, and documentation in full alignment with FDA SEND requirements, enabling faster, error-free submissions.

Our SEND services include:
  • ✔ SEND Dataset Creation Conversion and creation of SEND-compliant datasets from non-clinical toxicology studies.
  • ✔ Define.xml & Documentation Preparation of Define.xml files and reviewer’s guides for submission clarity.
  • ✔ Quality Control & Validation Rigorous QC and validation to ensure accuracy, consistency, and traceability.
  • ✔ Submission-Ready Delivery Final datasets packaged and delivered ready for regulatory submission.
Why Choose Our Non-Clinical Service ?
  • ✔ SEND Expertise Our team specializes in CDISC SEND standards, ensuring accurate and compliant data conversions.
  • ✔ Regulatory Assurance Adheres to the latest FDA and CDISC guidelines for complete compliance confidence.
  • ✔ Proven Workflow Automated tools and robust QC processes ensure high-quality, validated datasets.
  • ✔ Collaborative Approach We work closely with sponsors and CROs to deliver transparent, flexible, and on-time solutions.
  • ✔ Cost-Effective Delivery Optimized workflows reduce project time and costs without compromising quality.
