Accura

ACCURA is a Double data entry tool and is the sole purpose of double-checking that the information on the case record form is accurately entered into the database. Analysing exploratory data entails looking beyond the written data to test the plausibility of it. Accura analyses every single piece of data from every single study. Forms are designed by considering the user's perspective.

Key Features:
  • Validation of the data at each level
  • Verification of data by the second entry
  • Quality Assurance Of Data Occurrays
  • Audit Logs
  • Performance evaluations
  • eCRF Generation
  • Management of studies and visits
  • Provision of Developing Dynamic Forms
  • Process of data confirmation by Client
  • Rapid change management

DVE

Our Double Data Entry (DVE) tool offers a range of powerful features to enhance trial results. With rapid study start-up, simplified data management, and high security measures, our tool streamlines the entire data collection process. By ensuring accurate and reliable data through edit checks and comprehensive data capture modules, we enable researchers to make better-informed decisions.With DVE, researchers can confidently manage their studies, improve data flow, and ultimately contribute to more successful trial outcomes.

Key Features:
  • Rapid Study Start-Up
  • Simplified Data Management with High Security
  • Simplified User Management and Subject Allocation
  • Usability for All
  • Real-Time Trackability
  • Low Risk Study Modifications
